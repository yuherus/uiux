About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and effective technological solution for businesses, organizations, and individuals in tracking and managing key performance indicators (KPIs). We understand that effective KPI management is key to achieving great goals, and we aim to be a part of that journey with you.

Our Vision

We aim to develop an advanced, easy-to-use KPI management platform that meets all user needs. By combining academic knowledge with practical experience, we are confident that our product will deliver practical value and enhance work performance for all users.

Our Team

We are proud of our dynamic and diverse development team, comprising students from Information Technology, Business Administration, and Economics. Each member brings unique skills and knowledge, creating diversity and creativity in our product.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Constantly improving and updating to provide the best features for users.
  • Quality: Committed to delivering high-quality products that meet customer needs and expectations.
  • Collaboration: Always listening to and closely collaborating with users to improve the product.
  • Responsibility: Always prioritizing user benefits and acting with the highest sense of responsibility.
